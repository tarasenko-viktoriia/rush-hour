Multi-tenant Logistic System

Project Overview:

RushHour is an advanced multi-tenant logistics system designed to revolutionize the way truck driver deliveries are managed. Developed by our team, this platform integrates a powerful web portal for companies and a responsive Telegram bot for drivers, creating a seamless experience from order creation to delivery completion. The system is built to handle the complex logistics needs of multiple companies while ensuring scalability, security, and efficiency.

Technologies used:

Used the best technologies for the best result

Amazon S3

Utilized for scalable storage solutions, ensuring that all data, including delivery records, customer information, and invoices, are stored securely and can be accessed quickly.

ElasticSearch

Implemented to enhance search capabilities, allowing operators to efficiently search through large volumes of orders, invoices, and customer data.

.NET Core 6 & ASP.NET Core

The backbone of the system, providing a robust, high-performance environment for building and running the application. This ensures that the platform can handle multiple tenants without performance degradation.

ASP.NET Core Identity

Provides secure user authentication and management, ensuring that only authorized personnel can access sensitive data and system functionalities.

SignalR

Used to enable real-time communication between the web portal and the Telegram bot, ensuring that drivers receive instant updates on their delivery tasks and that companies can track deliveries as they happen.

Entity Framework & MySQL

Combined to manage the system's relational data, offering efficient data access and management while ensuring the reliability of the multi-tenant architecture.

Docker

Ensures consistent environments across development, testing, and production, allowing the system to be easily deployed and scaled.

Angular

Used to create a dynamic, responsive frontend for the web portal, offering a seamless and intuitive user experience for operators and administrators.

Key Features:

Dashboard

  • The dashboard offers a comprehensive view of overall activity, including delivery statuses, driver performance, and financial metrics.
  • Customizable widgets allow clients to tailor the dashboard to their specific needs, providing quick access to the most relevant information.
  • Visual data representations, such as graphs and charts, help users quickly interpret trends and make informed decisions.

Order list

  • A centralized hub where operators can access detailed information about every order, from its creation to its delivery.
  • Filters and search functionality allow users to easily locate specific orders based on various criteria, such as delivery status, customer name, or order date.
  • Each order entry includes comprehensive details, such as product descriptions, delivery instructions, assigned drivers, and associated invoices.

Dispatch

  • The dispatch module enables real-time tracking of deliveries, with a map interface showing the exact locations of trucks and their routes.
  • Dispatchers can monitor the progress of each delivery, receive real-time updates from drivers, and adjust routes or assignments as needed to optimize efficiency.
  • The module also includes detailed driver profiles, allowing dispatchers to assign tasks based on driver availability, expertise, and past performance.

Customer List

  • A complete database of customers, including contact details, delivery history, and satisfaction ratings.
  • The system allows companies to manage customer relationships more effectively by tracking interactions, preferences, and feedback.
  • Integrated rating systems help identify top customers and those who may need more attention, supporting better customer service and retention strategies.

Accounting

  • A robust accounting module that tracks all financial transactions, including customer invoices and payments.
  • Users can view each customer's billing history, generate new invoices, and record payments directly within the system.
  • The module supports multiple billing methods and currencies, making it suitable for businesses operating in different regions.

Outsource List

  • The outsource list manages information on external drivers and companies that can be engaged for delivery tasks.
  • The system supports seamless collaboration with third-party providers, ensuring that companies can scale their operations by tapping into external resources.
  • The module also tracks the performance of outsourced partners, helping companies maintain quality control and accountability.

Settings

  • The settings module provides extensive customization options, allowing companies to tailor the system to their specific operational needs.
  • Users can edit company information, configure billing methods, add or remove locations, and manage user accounts and permissions.
  • The module also supports localization features, enabling companies to adapt the system to different languages, time zones, and regulatory environments.

Project Outcomes

RushHour has dramatically improved the efficiency and effectiveness of logistics operations for the customers. The system's multi-tenant architecture allows different businesses to manage their logistics independently while benefiting from shared infrastructure. The web portal's user-friendly design and the Telegram bot's real-time capabilities have enhanced communication between operators and drivers, leading to faster and more accurate deliveries. The system's advanced features, such as real-time tracking, customizable dashboards, and integrated accounting, provide clients with a powerful toolset for managing their logistics operations.
